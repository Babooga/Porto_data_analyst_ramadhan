# Adidas Sales Performance Dashboard 

## Overview
This project presents an interactive sales performance dashboard built with **Power BI**, 
utilizing Adidas sales data across the United States. The dashboard provides a comprehensive 
view of sales performance, product distribution, and retailer contributions — enabling 
data-driven decision-making through clear and actionable visualizations.

---

## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze and visualize Adidas sales performance data 
to uncover key business insights, including revenue trends, regional distribution, 
product performance, and retailer effectiveness. This dashboard is designed to support 
strategic planning by translating raw sales data into meaningful visual narratives.

---

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following key metrics and visualizations:

### 📌 Key Metrics (KPI Cards)
* **Total Sales** — Overall revenue generated in USD
* **Average Operating Profit** — Mean profit after operational costs
* **Total Units Sold** — Cumulative number of units sold across all products
* **Average Price per Unit** — Mean selling price per individual product unit
* **Average Operating Margin** — Mean percentage of profit relative to total sales

### 📈 Sales Trends & Distribution
* **Monthly Sales Trend** — Total sales performance tracked month over month
* **Sales by Country** — Geographic breakdown of sales (United States)
* **Sales by Region** — Regional sales distribution across different U.S. areas
* **Sales by Product** — Revenue contribution broken down by each product category
* **Sales by Retailer** — Performance comparison across all partnered retail channels
